UBC Golf Club members help each other get better at the range and on the course. All skill levels are welcome!

What we do

Weekly Practices

Regular Thursday and Saturday golf range practices to fine-tune your game and gain tips from fellow members.

Group Tee Times

Join fellow student golfers for a round of golf during reserved times at local golf courses.

Heavy Discounts

Recieve sweet discounts on range tokens, green fees, golf merchandise and much more at local golf courses.

Free Rentals

Golf equipment is expensive. Members receive free daily rentals for range practices and golf rounds.
